Participant Data Summary

To sum up the data of our participants, we can safely assume that the website that has been built is not user-friendly. The most common reaction that was being shown was that the website was very chaotic and hard to navigate. This is a very understandable reaction, as the website does not have a clear structure as to what the website is about or any sort of user-friendliness to it. One participant did state that the website looks very similar to what you would see in a very old magazine print, and referenced the section of an old magazine that was showing job applications. This was a very interesting insight, as he was stating about how random the website was. Like an old magazine showing vacant job applications, there was no structure to how each job advertisement on a magazine was placed. If there was room, thats where the job advertisement would fit in.

Lessons learnt from Participant Research

We believe that we went about this research to the best of our abilities. We believe we asked all the right questions to get the best information possible out of the participants. We can take this information for future research projects as we now know what kind of questions we would need to ask if we were to get some reliable information out of our research participants.
